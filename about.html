ABOUT US

Webster Dudley Food share is a non-profit organization that provide essential food to all Webster and Dudley residents in need.

Impressium

Webster Dudley Food Share (WDFS) is a non-profit organization. Webster Dudley Food Share maintains affiliation as a member agency of the Massachusetts Food Bank Network. WDFS is lead by President James Alkire, Vice President Mary-Jane Sullivan, Treasurer James Steele and Secretary Maryanne Hickey; The organization also has a number of dedicated and loyal volunteers.
